What a joy to play in such a setting. Over a hundred people came to attend the concert. The soft piano music blended with the spectacular scenery of the Pyrenees range and the Mauvezin Castle—everything was there!

## Location

I am offering a piano concert at 7 PM on May 23 in front of the Mauvezin Castle, featuring a view of the Pyrenees range.

Unlike other nature concerts where I am dependent on good weather, we have a fallback solution at the village church in case of bad weather.

Please park in the lower parking lot and reach the castle on foot (100 m).

The concert will last about 1 hour and 15 minutes and will be followed by a small drink/aperitif.

Additionally, you will be seated on the grass; those who wish to do so should feel free to bring a camping chair or other seating.

Pay what you want.
