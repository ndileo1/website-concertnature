## Details

- **Date**: Friday, 23th May 2026
- **Heure**: 6 PM
- **Location** : Mauvezin, Hautes-Pyrénées

{{< figure src="/images/cn mauvezin.jpeg">}}

After last year’s edition, which was a huge success, I propose you a new concert with a completely new repertoire. 
Don’t miss this extraordinary event in an absolutely unforgettable setting!!!
Can’t wait for you to come back and share my music with you.

The event is organized by the association "la Marguerite de Mauvezin (65130)", "l'escola Gaston Febus"

Unlike other nature concerts where I am dependent on good weather, we have a fallback solution at the village church in case of bad weather.

You will have to park in the lower parking lot and reach the castle on foot (100 m). 

The concert will last about 1 hour and will be followed by a small drink. 

Moreover, you will be settled in the grass. For those who want, don’t hesitate to take a camping chair, blanket or other. 


Open participation.
